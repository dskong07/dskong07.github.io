Areas of Interest

Here are some of my favorite things to work on!

Data Analytics

I love uncovering patterns in raw data—whether it’s cleaning messy datasets or exploring trends in Census data. My work spans from preprocessing pipelines to actionable insights that drive decisions.

Machine Learning

From fine-tuning transformers for semantic segmentation to developing real-time object detection models, I enjoy pushing ML systems from theory to practical applications.

Visualization

Turning complex data into intuitive stories is a science and an art. From building interactive dashboards to geospatial visualizations, my goal is to paint an intuitive and accessible picture of the data underneath.

Data Processing

Building efficient pipelines is important too! The data has to come from somewhere, after all. I've designed data flows to track staging, deployment, and live testing data, while focusing on scalability, automation, and ensuring clean, actionable data reaches those who need it.

Computer Vision

The way machines interpret visuals is so fascinating! While seeing is a given for humans, computers parse matrices of raw numbers—which allows us to do incredible stuff like real-time object detection in videos or delineating components of images.

Quantitative Modeling

A more personal venture, I've always enjoyed the intersection of mathematic models and data when it comes to decisionmaking. From strategy research to algorithmic trading systems, I've been diving into the depths of the quantitative finance world headfirst.
