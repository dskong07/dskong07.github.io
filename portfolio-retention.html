Retention Map Details

An Interactive U.S. Education Retention Map.

I designed an interactive map visualizing student retention rates across U.S. states, segmented by gender, race, and school level (elementary, middle, high). The goal is to highlight disparities in educational outcomes and encourage users to explore how demographics influence retention trends.

Using the U.S. state map as a primary visualization, retention rates are encoded via color intensityâ€”darker shades indicating higher dropout proportions. To enhance interactivity, I implemented dynamic filters (dropdown menus for gender, race, and grade level) and intuitive color associations (blue for male, pink for female, purple for combined). Users can hover for state-specific summaries or click to lock selections and view detailed metrics.
